How It Works
Wolf Garden Rentals

Rent world-class optics, night vision, and outdoor gear from verified owners — shipped to your door. We built this the right way so both sides are protected, especially when we're talking $5,000 spotting scopes and $10,000 night vision rigs. Read this once and you'll know exactly how it works.

For the Renter
📦 Renting Gear
1 — Book & Pay

Select your dates and check out. You pay the daily rental rate plus a fully refundable security deposit. The deposit is held by Stripe — not by the owner — and released back to your card once the owner confirms clean return.

2 — Gear Arrives, Photograph It

When the box arrives, photograph everything immediately before you use it — every surface, any existing scratches or wear. Click the photo upload link in your confirmation email and submit them. This is your protection from being held responsible for pre-existing damage.

3 — Use It, Return It

Treat it like your own high-end gear. Pack it exactly as it arrived. The owner includes a prepaid return label inside the box — use it. Drop at any UPS location on or before the last rental day.

4 — Get Your Deposit Back

Once the owner confirms the gear came back in the same condition, Wolf Garden will process your deposit release. Funds return to your card within 5–10 business days via Stripe. Contact wolfgarden21@gmail.com to initiate the return confirmation.

For the Owner
🔭 Renting Out Gear
1 — List Your Gear

Set your daily rate, minimum and maximum rental days, security deposit amount, and the full replacement value of the gear. Be honest about condition — your reputation depends on it. Wolf Garden takes 18% of rental revenue to cover the platform and shipping labels both ways.

2 — Photograph Before Shipping

This is the most important step. Before you pack the gear, photograph every surface in good lighting — top, bottom, all sides, lenses on and off, any existing wear. Upload via the link in your notification email. No photos on file means no damage claim coverage. Full stop.

3 — Ship Within 24 Hours

Pack it well — use the original case if you have it. Include the prepaid return label inside the box. Ship within 24 hours of the booking start date and share tracking through Wolf Garden messaging.

4 — Confirm Return & Get Paid

When the gear comes back, photograph it and compare to your pre-ship photos. If everything checks out, email wolfgarden21@gmail.com to confirm the return. Your payout deposits to your bank within 2–5 business days via Stripe.

Both Sides
🛡️ Deposits & Disputes
How Deposits Work

The owner sets the deposit — typically 20–30% of replacement cost. It is charged at booking and held by Stripe. It is not paid to the owner unless a damage claim is approved. It returns to the renter automatically on clean return confirmation.

If There's Damage

The owner has 48 hours after confirmed delivery to file a damage report with photos. Wolf Garden compares the owner's pre-ship photos against the renter's arrival photos and the owner's post-return photos. The photo record is the evidence — both sides are protected by it.

Wear vs Damage

Dust, field smudges, and normal use marks are expected and not claimable. Cracked lenses, broken dials, missing caps, dents, and water damage are claimable. Owners who list gear as mint when it isn't will find the photo record works against them.

High Value Gear

For gear over $2,000 in replacement value we strongly recommend owners carry a personal articles insurance policy covering the equipment while in others' possession. Wolf Garden is not an insurance provider and the deposit may not cover full replacement cost on premium optics or night vision.

Our Story

Rudy — Founder of Wolf Garden

Wolf Garden was born in the Arizona desert by a hunter and outdoorsman who volunteered with one of the most versatile search and rescue teams in the country — Southern Arizona Search and Rescue — and was tired of overpaying for gear, getting burned on sketchy Facebook Marketplace deals, and having nowhere purpose-built for the outdoor community to buy, sell, and trade.

We built the marketplace we always wanted — one that speaks our language, respects our community, and gives hunters, anglers, and hikers a safe and trusted place to do business with each other.

FAQ
Straight answers to the most common questions.

Buying

Is it safe to buy here?
All payments go through Stripe — the same payment processor used by Amazon and major retailers. Your card info is never stored on Wolf Garden. Seller ratings and reviews are visible on every listing so you can see who you're buying from before you commit.

What if the item isn't as described?
You have 48 hours after receiving an item to flag a problem through the messaging system. If the item is significantly not as described, Stripe's buyer dispute process protects you. See our Disputes page for the full process.

How does shipping work?
Shipping is arranged directly between buyer and seller. Once a sale is agreed on, the seller ships the item and provides a tracking number through the Wolf Garden messaging system. We recommend comparing rates at USPS, UPS, and FedEx before shipping. Wolf Garden does not handle shipping directly.

How do trades work?
Trades are completely free — Wolf Garden charges zero fees. You and the seller agree on the trade through messaging, then ship to each other. Both parties pay their own shipping. Simple as that.

Selling

How much does it cost to sell?
Free to list. Wolf Garden takes a 5% fee only when your item sells. No monthly fees, no subscription, no upfront cost.

How do I get paid?
Stripe deposits your payout directly to your bank account within 2 business days of a completed sale.

Can I sell anything?
Outdoor gear only — hunting, fishing, camping, hiking, climbing, archery, optics, and search & rescue equipment. No firearms, no ammunition, no illegal items. Wolf Garden reserves the right to remove any listing that doesn't fit the community.

Renting

How does renting work?
The owner ships the item with a prepaid return label included. You pay a refundable security deposit upfront which is returned when the item comes back in the same condition. Phone verification is required to complete any rental booking.

What if I damage a rented item?
The security deposit covers minor wear. For significant damage, the renter is responsible for the cost of repair or replacement up to the item's listed replacement value. This is agreed to at the time of booking.

Account

How do I report a bad listing?
Hit the Report button on any listing. We review all reports and remove listings that violate our terms.

How do I delete my account?
Email wolfgarden21@gmail.com and we'll remove your account and all data within 48 hours.

Buyer Protection
What we cover and what we don't — straight talk.
Wolf Garden facilitates transactions between private parties. We are not a party to any sale, trade, or rental between buyers and sellers.

What Protects You

Stripe Payment Security
Every purchase goes through Stripe. Your card information is never stored on Wolf Garden. If you pay and receive nothing, you can dispute the charge through your bank or credit card — that process exists and works independently of Wolf Garden.

Seller Ratings & Reviews
Every seller has a visible rating and review history. Always check a seller's reputation before buying. New sellers with no history carry more risk — factor that into your decision.

Secure Messaging
Use Wolf Garden's built-in messaging to ask questions before you buy. Get clarity on condition, shipping timeline, and anything else that matters. A seller who won't answer questions is a red flag.

Report System
Every listing has a Report button. Use it if something looks wrong. We review all reports and remove listings that violate our terms.

Phone Verification on Rentals
All rental bookings require phone verification. This eliminates anonymous bad actors from the rental side of the platform.

What We Don't Cover

He Said She Said Disputes
Wolf Garden does not mediate disagreements between buyers and sellers about item condition, shipping delays, or personal disputes. These go through Stripe's dispute process or are resolved between the parties directly.

Items Not as Described
Wolf Garden encourages honest listings and penalizes bad sellers through the review system. However, we cannot guarantee the accuracy of every listing. Use your judgment, ask questions, and check seller ratings before buying.

Best Practices

Always message the seller first. Check their rating. Read their reviews. Ask for additional photos if needed. Never pay outside of Wolf Garden's checkout. If a deal feels off, trust your gut — the outdoor community has a reputation to uphold and most sellers here are genuine.

Disputes
How to handle problems when they come up.
Wolf Garden is a platform that connects buyers and sellers. We are not a party to transactions. Most disputes are best resolved directly between buyer and seller — that's always the fastest path.

Step 1 — Talk to Each Other

Use Wolf Garden's messaging system to contact the other party directly. Most issues — shipping delays, minor condition questions, rental return timing — get resolved in one conversation. The outdoor community is generally straight with each other.

Step 2 — Flag the Issue

If direct messaging doesn't resolve it, use the Report button on the listing. Describe the problem clearly. We review all reports and will remove sellers who are operating in bad faith from the platform.

Step 3 — Stripe Dispute (Purchases Only)

For payment disputes — if you paid for something and received nothing, or received something completely different from what was listed — contact your bank or credit card company to initiate a chargeback through Stripe. This is the proper channel for payment-related disputes and it works independently of Wolf Garden.

You have 48 hours after receiving an item to flag a condition issue before leaving a review. After that window, the transaction is considered complete.

For Rentals

Rental disputes about item condition are handled through the security deposit. The owner documents item condition with photos before shipping. The renter documents condition on receipt. Disputes about damage compare those two records. If there's a genuine disagreement, contact us at wolfgarden21@gmail.com with both sets of photos and we'll help mediate.

What Wolf Garden Will Do

We will remove sellers who receive consistent bad reports. We will ban users who are found to be operating in bad faith. We will assist in rental damage disputes where both parties have provided photo documentation. We will respond to all flagged reports.

What Wolf Garden Won't Do

We won't issue refunds directly — that goes through Stripe. We won't mediate he said she said disputes with no documentation. We won't get involved in trade disputes where both parties agreed to the trade and shipped — those are private agreements.
